"VUU Alert"
Coding Project

"VUU Alert" is an application me and another student decided to develop in order to better the communication between RA's and Students. Currently, the way the communcations are set up, is that students, RA's and housing directors are all in a GroupMe groupchat or groupchats via text message. Even though these may be good channels of communication, it's not at all effective. Especially when it comes to documenting incidents or events that happen and require immediate response. The main idea is - if there's an issue, the student would then be able to go to the app, click a button, and it will allow them to send a notification to the RA on duty, and the RA on duty (who has to be logged into the app while on duty) will then be able to see and repond the request more efficiently as well as everything being documented and saved in our databases. This project is currently in it's early stages of development, but it is underway!
